02 — Process Behind My Content

How My Content Is Generated

Step 01

Brainstorming & Research

My process begins by blending AI-driven brainstorming with current trends across social media to identify relevant tech topics. From there, I prioritize technical integrity. By cross-referencing AI insights with official documentation and verified technical data, I deconstruct complex systems into precise, accurate narratives that maintain a high standard of educational quality.

Step 02

Script & Storyboarding

After the research phase, I transform technical data into a structured, easy-to-follow script. I design each storyboard to prioritize clarity and viewer retention, breaking down sophisticated topics into bite-sized segments. By focusing on simple, relatable language, I ensure that every video is accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical background.

Step 03

Production & Editing Process

During recording, I bring a dynamic and confident energy to the mic, creating a professional yet conversational tone that keeps viewers watching. I then bring the topic to life by leveraging AI tools to generate high-end, realistic visuals. Rather than adding random effects, every scene is crafted to drive the story forward and help the user easily visualize how the tech works.

Step 04

Publish & Iterate

Once production is complete, the content goes live on YouTube and Instagram. I actively review user comments and video metrics to learn what resonates best with the audience. This cycle of publishing and analyzing feedback helps me continuously improve and boost the performance of future videos.
